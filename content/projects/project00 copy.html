8bit computer

Although the computer does have some odd signal connectivity issues at the moment, it has proven to work as a functional digital system in the past and that's good enough for me. I plan on building another computer in the near future which takes care of some of the issues present in this version and makes several improvements to the previous design, such as a video card, an interrupt module and a 16 bit architecture. I will also be looking into ordering a custom PCB to cut down on assemble time costs + connectivity issues. Overall, building the computer has been a rigorous, informative and rewarding project which I wish to attempt again.
