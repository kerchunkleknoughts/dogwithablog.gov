I had a dream that I was on a cruise ship or something of the sort, I was with a team of people tasked with shutting down a criminal or something. Spy stuff. Or maybe it was just a large party I don't remember, there was a scene where I was climbing outside the building by holding onto the bricks of the building and spying in the windows into a large hall. The large hall had a party going on in it. [redacted] was there. He was dressed up in vacation clothes. He was glad to see me again but it was the sort if glad that was tinted with distrust. I was talking to him a little, he offered a drink to me, I didn't want it because I knew I would feel like shit in the morning if I drank . Then everyone started dancing in the large hall, I joined with one drink in my hands and started making up dance moves since I didn't know how to dance. [redacted] saw and I was slightly embarrassed I think he had a girlfriend, there was a scene where we were talking where his girl came over and cozied up to him while we talked. That's when I was trying to ask how he'd been and how his life was . I remember feeling a sting of jealousy because he had a girl and I didn't. He seemed like his life was great and figured out while I was just coming back for scraps of a social life, and I was the pathetic one, I was like a beggar in tattered clothes pleading him to help despite the fact that I had turned my back on everyone and ghosted them.
